   - IMPORTANT: Use `requestPasswordReset` NOT `forgotPassword` (Pitfall 7 — v1.4 breaking change renamed the function)
   - Always show success message regardless of whether email exists (locked decision: no account existence leakage)

   - On success: redirect to /login with "Password reset successfully" message
   - On error (expired/invalid token): show error with link back to /forgot-password
