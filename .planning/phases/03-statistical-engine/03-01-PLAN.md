**packages/db/src/schema/seasonal-events.ts:**

**packages/db/src/schema/saturation-estimates.ts:**
- Table `saturation_estimates` with all columns from RESEARCH.md schema (id, tenantId, campaignId, estimatedAt, saturationPct, hillAlpha, hillMu, hillGamma, status)
